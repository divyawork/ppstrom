FIBONACCI SERIES

What is Fibonacci Series?

The Fibonacci series is a sequence of numbers in which each number is the sum of the two preceding ones, usually starting with 0 and 1.

Fn = F(n-1) + F(n-2)

History

The Fibonacci sequence is named after the Italian mathematician Leonardo Fibonacci, who introduced it to the Western world in his book Liber Abaci, published in 1202.

Applications

The Fibonacci sequence has applications in various fields, including mathematics, computer science, art, nature and closely related to the golden ratio.

Connection with Nature

The Fibonacci sequence appears in various aspects of nature, such as the arrangement of leaves on a stem, the formation of pinecones and pineapples, and the spirals of sunflowers.
